Supply
EXCHANGE: Cadets with ill-fitting uniform parts can submit requests for exchange for a size that fits from the dashboard: Click Exchange at the bottom of the dashboard under Supply.

Please note: pick up will be at the Squadron Office on the Supply night. If parts are not in stock, you will be notified when the parts will be ready to pick up.

Supply Nights will be on Thursday of each month, Sept 11th, Sept 25th, Oct 23rd, Nov 20th and Dec 11th between 6:30 pm to 8:30 pm. Cadets can drop by without advance request, but no guarantee that the uniform parts are in stock. Please bring back the washed uniform to exchange.

RETURN: Cadets no longer with the squadron should return their washed uniform to supply. Returns are accepted every Tuesday during regular training at Bur Oak Secondary School. Drop off the uniform on the Supply Desk. Please include the return form.

Cadets with outstanding supply requests are to follow up with the Supply WO.
