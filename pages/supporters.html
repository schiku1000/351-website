Our Valued Supporters

The 351 Silver Star Squadron thrives thanks to the incredible support from our community partners, volunteers, and sponsors.

Financial Support

The 351 Silver Star Squadron cannot operate without the active support from the community. While the Canadian government funds the basic operational costs, the squadron relies on ongoing support from local community to continue the high quality training activities and services we offer our cadets at no cost to them.

To become a financial supporter of the 351 Silver Star Squadron, please contact the squadron sponsoring committee.

WO Alfred Wong Memorial Scholarship

The Warrant Officer Alfred Wong of 351 (Silver Star) Squadron Memorial Scholarship was created in 2007 in memory of this exceptional young man and outstanding cadet.

For a Serving or Former Cadet Entering a Post-Secondary Music-related Program, or Enrolling in a Post-Secondary Program who was a Member of a Squadron Band for at Least Three Years.

This $1,200 scholarship is granted yearly to a deserving Air Cadet on a merit basis.

Qualifying instructions and award application can be found on the scholarship package.
